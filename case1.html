The Case of Thomas Maurer

Thomas Maurer, a 28-year-old American man, disappeared near Varna Airport in Bulgaria while vacationing with friends at the Golden Sands resort. According to airport records, Maurer fled from a medical consultation at the airport and vanished into a nearby forest.

According to the investigation, Maurer fled from the airport medical office after noticing the arrival of another man who was part of the airport construction team. Witnesses reported that Maurer ran through the main terminal and climbed over the security fence. The last sighting of him was as he attempted to enter a forest near the highway.

The investigation began the day after Mauer’s disappearance when his mother contacted the police to report his strange behavior and disappearance. Investigators searched the airport and hotels in the city, but they couldn’t locate the boy. Airport staff who had interacted with Mauer were interviewed. The doctor, who supposedly had the last conversation with Mauer, reported that he appeared nervous and kept repeating that he didn’t want to die there. According to the doctor, Mauer fled upon seeing a man dressed as a construction worker in the airport clinic. The surveillance footage from that day was handed over to the forensic team.

Mauer’s friends, who were on vacation with him, reported that a few days before, he had arrived at the hotel with several bruises. When questioned, he claimed to have been involved in a bar fight with football fans from an opposing team. His friends returned to their home country five days before Mauer.
